📚 User Guide

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Capture Screen: Click "🖥️ Capture Screen" to capture your entire screen
  2. Capture Area: Click "✂️ Capture Area" to select a specific region
  3. Upload Image: Click "📁 Upload Image" to import an existing image

✏️ Annotation Tools

  • ➡️ Arrow: Draw arrows to point at important areas
  • ▭ Rectangle: Draw rectangular boxes
  • ○ Circle: Draw circles around elements
  • ✨ Highlight: Highlight text or areas with semi-transparent color
  • 🔒 Blur: Pixelate sensitive information (can be undone!)
  • T Text: Add text annotations

🎨 Customization

  • Use the color picker to change annotation colors
  • Adjust the slider to change line thickness
  • Press ↩️ Undo to remove the last annotation
  • Press 🗑️ Clear to remove all annotations

💾 Saving & Sharing

  • 💾 Download: Save the annotated image to your device
  • 📤 Share/Copy: Share via system dialog or copy to clipboard

⚡ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Z: Undo last annotation
  • Escape: Close modals or cancel operations

🔒 Privacy & Security

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your screenshots and annotations remain private on your device.
